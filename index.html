        What is this?

        This is a lightweight Khan Academy video viewer app. When I first visited the Khan Academy website, I was very curious to check out some of videos. However, I found the list of videos to be a little overwhelming. (The site design has improved greatly since then!) When I learned about the Khan Academy APIs, I decided to make a random video viewer, so that it would take very little effort to get started.

        It has since evolved into a generic viewer app that make it very easy to explore the content on Khan Academy. While you can browse through the different topics, I still think the most interesting videos are the ones you stumble upon by choosing a random video. (Click on the dial button on the TV!)
